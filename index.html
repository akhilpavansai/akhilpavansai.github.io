Welcome To Art Gallery

An Online Gallery About Modern & Contemporary Indian Art
Masters Collection is over a decade old Indian Art Gallery based in Kolkata, India. The primary vision of the gallery is to procure, advice & promote an exclusive range of Indian Contemporary Art, Indian Modern Art and all other genres of Indian Painting by exploring and evaluating artists from across India.
Over the last few years, Indian Art has gained immense fame not only on national grounds but also on an international level. And it is with the aim to continue the respect and charm, that this gallery currently showcases some of the finest collection of Indian Artworks & Indian Paintings of eminent and famous artists of Bengal & rest of India.
